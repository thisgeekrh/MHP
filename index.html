Military Home Partners

Who We Are

Military Home Partners is made up of a group of well established real estate professionals in the state of South Carolina. We are made up of unique and strong willed individuals, who are ready to help you find or sell your home. We have experience working with veterans such as yourself and we understand the process of purchasing a home with a VA loan type. Our strategic partners can help you get pre-approved for your next adventure to being a home owner.

How it Works

Once you complete our short contact form with your contact information, our inside client services agent will reach out to you and connect you with our local lenders to get your pre-approval process started. From there, that same client services agent will connect you with one of our experienced real estate agents and we will start your home search together.

Our real estate agents will provide insight on current market trends, information on houses you want to view, persuasive negotiations, the writing of the contract, and all the other small steps until you get to the closing table with the keys to your new home in hand. Home buying is a BIG DEAL, but with our experienced agents and with our knowledge of VA loans we will make it as easy as possible. We are here with you every step of the way and our goal is to ensure your happiness in the home buying experience, all while providing you exponential service along the way.
